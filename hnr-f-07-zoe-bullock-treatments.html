Zoe Bullock is an artist from Portland, Oregon working in film, performance, and writing. Her work sites theater and literature as a space of respite for honesty from a society that uses oppression and the disposal of life for profit.
She is currently living in New York City and writing a feature length script. 'Treatments' is a collection of scripts and ephemera from Zoe’s most recent works starring Katerina Tannenbaum and herself.
