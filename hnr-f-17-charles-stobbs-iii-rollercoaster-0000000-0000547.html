Rollercoaster 0000000 - 0000547 is the first in a series of publications by Stobbs dedicated to the reconstruction of Rollercoaster (1977) through print. This edition presents the first 547 frames of the film, scaled and arranged under the conditions of the book as to introduce a temporal distortion within the flow of images. Subsequent frames will continue to be published in concurrence with the rest of Stobbs' life.

Charles Stobbs III is an artist, writer, and intermittent educator. All self published works can be found at modifiedguilt.com and all works made with Neo Gibson can be found at frequencymodulation.pictures.
